## Controls
- **Left Click** = Reveal cell
- **Right Click** = Flag cell
- **Shift + Click** = Chord (reveal neighbors)
- **Smiley Button** = Restart game

## First Game Walkthrough

1. **Choose Difficulty**: Click "Beginner" (9Ã—9, 10 mines) for your first game
2. **First Click**: Click any cell - it's guaranteed safe!
3. **Read Numbers**: Numbers show how many mines are adjacent
4. **Flag Mines**: Right-click cells you think are mines
5. **Reveal Safe Cells**: Left-click cells you know are safe
6. **Win**: Reveal all non-mine cells!

## Tips
- Start from corners for easier games
- Numbers + flags = guaranteed safe cells nearby
- Use chord clicking (shift+click) on numbers to save time
- Watch the mine counter to track your progress
