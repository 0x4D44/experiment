## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Space** - Start/Pause
- **S** - Step
- **C** - Clear
- **R** - Random
- **+** - Zoom in
- **-** - Zoom out

## Mouse Controls

- **Click** - Toggle cell
- **Drag** - Draw multiple cells
- **Shift + Drag** - Pan view
- **Mouse Wheel** - Zoom
