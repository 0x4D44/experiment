## Game Objective

Stack falling tetromino pieces to create complete horizontal lines.
Completed lines disappear and earn you points.
Don't let the pieces stack to the top!

## Tips for High Scores

1. **Go for Tetris** - Clear 4 lines at once for maximum points (800 √ó level)
2. **Use Ghost Piece** - The transparent preview shows where pieces will land
3. **Check Next Piece** - Plan ahead using the preview panel
4. **Hard Drop Mastery** - Press SPACE for instant placement
