STRATEGY TIPS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. CENTER CONTROL
   The center column (column 4) provides the most opportunities
   for creating four-in-a-row combinations.

2. BUILD FROM BOTTOM
   Don't leave gaps that your opponent can exploit. Stack pieces
   strategically from the bottom up.

3. CREATE THREATS
   Try to set up situations where you can win in multiple ways,
   forcing your opponent to make impossible choices.

4. WATCH DIAGONALS
   Diagonal wins are easy to miss but just as effective as
   horizontal or vertical wins.

5. ALWAYS BLOCK
   If your opponent has three in a row, blocking is mandatory!
   Missing a block is the most common way to lose.

6. THINK AHEAD
   Consider not just the current move, but how it sets up your
   next moves and limits your opponent's options.

  - Evaluates game tree to find optimal moves
  - Alpha-beta pruning reduces search space by 60-90%
  - Position evaluation with sophisticated heuristics
  - Move ordering for optimal pruning efficiency

ARCHITECTURE: Clean, Modular Design
  - board.rs: Pure game logic (no UI/AI coupling)
  - ai.rs: AI algorithms (uses board for evaluation)
  - ui.rs: Terminal rendering (no game logic)
  - game.rs: Orchestrates board, AI, and UI
  - main.rs: Entry point and initialization

TESTING: Comprehensive Coverage
